What is Water Scarcity?

Water scarcity refers to the lack of sufficient available water resources to meet the demands of usage in a region. It impacts health, livelihood, and the environment.

Main Causes

  • Climate change leading to droughts
  • Pollution of freshwater sources
  • Overuse in agriculture & industry
  • Poor infrastructure & management
